 # 📊 Blinkit Sales Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes Blinkit's grocery sales data using Power BI to uncover business insights and visualize key performance metrics. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales performance, outlet efficiency, product category trends, and customer ratings through interactive visualizations and KPI tracking.

The goal of this project is to transform raw sales data into actionable insights that support data-driven business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The primary objective of this analysis is to evaluate Blinkit's sales performance across different outlet types, outlet sizes, product categories, and geographical locations.

Key business questions addressed include:

* Which product categories generate the highest sales?
* Which outlet types contribute the most revenue?
* How does sales performance vary across outlet sizes and city tiers?
* What trends can be observed in outlet establishment and growth?
* How do customer ratings relate to overall sales performance?

## 📊 Dashboard Analysis

### 1. Sales by Item Type

The dashboard analyzes sales across multiple product categories including:

* Fruits & Vegetables
* Snack Foods
* Household Items
* Frozen Foods
* Dairy Products
* Canned Goods
* Baking Goods
* Health & Hygiene
* Meat
* Soft Drinks

#### Key Findings

* Fruits & Vegetables generated the highest sales.
* Snack Foods ranked as the second-best performing category.
* Seafood and Breakfast products contributed the lowest sales.

---

### 2. Fat Content Analysis

Products are categorized into:

* Low Fat
* Regular

#### Key Findings

* Low Fat products generated slightly higher sales than Regular products.
* Consumer demand shows a strong preference for healthier product options.

---

### 3. Outlet Size Analysis

Sales performance is analyzed across:

* Small Outlets
* Medium Outlets
* High Outlets

#### Key Findings

| Outlet Size | Sales   |
| ----------- | ------- |
| Medium      | $507.9K |
| Small       | $444.8K |
| High        | $249.0K |

Medium-sized outlets contributed the highest revenue share.

---

### 4. Outlet Location Analysis

The dashboard compares sales across:

* Tier 1 Cities
* Tier 2 Cities
* Tier 3 Cities

#### Key Findings

| Tier   | Sales   |
| ------ | ------- |
| Tier 3 | $472.1K |
| Tier 2 | $393.2K |
| Tier 1 | $336.4K |

Tier 3 locations generated the highest sales among all outlet locations.

---

### 5. Outlet Establishment Trend

This analysis tracks outlet performance based on establishment year.

#### Key Findings

* Significant sales growth is observed after 2016.
* Peak sales exceeded $200K around 2018.
* Established outlets consistently contribute higher revenue.

---

### 6. Outlet Type Performance

The dashboard evaluates different outlet formats.

| Outlet Type        | Total Sales |
| ------------------ | ----------- |
| Supermarket Type 1 | $787.5K     |
| Grocery Store      | $151.9K     |
| Supermarket Type 2 | $131.5K     |
| Supermarket Type 3 | $130.7K     |

#### Key Findings

* Supermarket Type 1 dominates overall sales performance.
* Grocery Stores contribute significantly less revenue compared to larger supermarket formats.

---

## 📌 Key Business Insights

### Revenue Drivers

* Fruits & Vegetables and Snack Foods are the highest revenue-generating categories.
* Medium-sized outlets produce the largest share of sales.

### Geographic Performance

* Tier 3 locations outperform Tier 1 and Tier 2 cities in total sales.
* Regional demand varies significantly by outlet location.

### Outlet Efficiency

* Supermarket Type 1 is the most effective outlet format.
* Larger retail formats demonstrate stronger sales performance.

### Customer Satisfaction

* Average customer rating remains stable at 3.92.
* High-performing outlets generally maintain consistent ratings.

### Growth Trends

* Sales growth accelerated with the expansion of outlet establishments.
* Long-established outlets contribute significantly to overall revenue.
